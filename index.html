Last updated: [시행일 : July 6, 2026]

This privacy policy explains how LINEZIP ("the App") handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require account creation, login, or personal identification. The App may process the following limited data:

  • Advertising data: The App uses Google AdMob to display ads, which may collect advertising identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID) for ad delivery and measurement.
  • Purchase data: If you make an in-app purchase, Google Play Billing processes the transaction. We do not receive or store your payment details.
  • Locally stored progress: Game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device (and may be included in device backups) and are not transmitted to us.

2. How We Use Information

Data collected via AdMob is used solely to display and measure advertising, in accordance with Google's own privacy practices. We do not sell personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which may collect information as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Billing — https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

5. Data Retention

Locally stored game data remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data. We do not retain this data on any server.

6. Your Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's Ads settings (Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization").

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
